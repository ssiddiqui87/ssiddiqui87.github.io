Some projects I have worked on

Here are some projects I would like to highlight from my portfolio. The first one is an Android app I created as part of a term project with 4 other people in one of my classes at Wayne State University. The basic idea behind the app is to have a crowd sourced list of restaurant menus so users can go to one app to look up various menus for different restaurants rather than going to each website individually.

The second one is a lab we worked on in Grand Circus with partners. We had to make an app that acted as a car lot that stored both new and used cars.
